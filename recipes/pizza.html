Pizza

This is a pepperoni pizza recipe with a deep pan base.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato pizza topper
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Method

  1. Prepare dough
  2. Spoon over even layer of pizza topper
  3. Spinkle a good helping of mozzerella
  4. Add Pepperoni
  5. Cook at 200 degrees C for 20 minutes
