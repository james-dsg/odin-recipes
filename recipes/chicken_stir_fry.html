Chicken Stir Fry

A succulent chicken stir fry, a true mid-week favourite.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • Noodles
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Herbs and spices

Method

  1. Cook chicken
  2. Saute onions and bell peppers
  3. Add herbs and spices
  4. Add cooked noodles
  5. Stir all together and cook for 5 mins
